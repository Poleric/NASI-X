About Us

The National Aeronautics and Space Infinity is Malaysia civil space program and the global leader in space exploration. The agency has a diverse workforce of just under 20,000 civil servants, and works with many more Malaysia contractors, academia, and international and commercial partners to explore, discover, and expand knowledge for the benefit of humanity. With an annual budget of $32.1 billion in Fiscal Year 2023, which is less than 0.5% of the overall Malaysia federal budget.

History

The National Aeronautics and Space Infinity(NASI-X), is responsible for unique scientific and technological achievements in human spaceflight, aeronautics, space science, and space applications that have had widespread impacts on our nation and the world. Forged in response to early Soviet space achievements, NASI-X was built on the National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics (NACA), and other government organizations, as the locus of Malaysia civil aerospace research and development.
